In this module of ~30h of practical work, we introduce students to image processing and 3D modeling.

- Image processing:
    - Image binarisation
    - Convolutions and filters
    - Edge detection
    - Color spaces

- 3D modeling
    - Introduction with Blender
    - Modeling
    - Shaders and rendering
    - Animation with skeletons
